Who Am I?

I'm Abrar Shakil, a passionate full-stack web developer from Bangladesh with over 3 years of professional experience in building modern web applications. I specialize in creating elegant solutions to complex problems using cutting-edge technologies.

My passion for web development started during my academic journey at National University of Bangladesh. While pursuing my degree in Economics, I discovered the power of technology and decided to pursue web development.
